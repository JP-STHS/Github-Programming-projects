Welcome to raise a rock!
CONTROLS:
Press the left and right arrows to move your rock around.
Pet your rock by clicking with the left mouse button.
Press the disco button to change the scenery and music. (Does not effect the status of the rock.)
Press the Burger button to feed your rock.
Press "Q" to let your rock have fun.


Feed your rock, give your rock the perfect amount of care, and let your rock have just enough fun in this mind-blowing game, Raise a Rock.

The point is simple; Let your rock reach adulthood without dying!
